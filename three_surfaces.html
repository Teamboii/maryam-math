What shape is the surface?

The angles of a triangle tell you — this is what Maryam Mirzakhani spent her career exploring

Flat surface
60° + 60° + 60°
= 180° ✓
always exactly 180°
Sphere (globe)
90° + 90° + 90°
= 270° !
more than 180°
Saddle (hyperbolic)
45° + 45° + 45°
= 135°
less than 180°

The angle sum of a triangle tells you what kind of surface you're on. Maryam Mirzakhani worked on hyperbolic surfaces — saddle-shaped spaces where strange, beautiful math happens.
